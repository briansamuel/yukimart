Test Instructions

Setup:

  1. Make sure you're logged into the system (yukimart@gmail.com / 123456)
  2. Resize your browser window to approximately 800px width to force horizontal scrolling
  3. Use the iframe below to test the invoice list page

Manual Test Steps

Step 1: Load and Expand Panel

1. Click "Load Invoice Page" button above

2. Wait for the page to load completely

3. Click on any invoice row to expand the detail panel

4. Verify the panel appears below the clicked row

Step 2: Test Horizontal Scroll

1. With the detail panel open, scroll the table horizontally using:

  • Horizontal scrollbar at the bottom of the table
  • Shift + mouse wheel
  • Touch/trackpad horizontal swipe

2. Observe that the detail panel stays fixed at the left edge

3. The table content should scroll behind the panel

Step 3: Test Extreme Scroll Positions

1. Scroll the table to the maximum right position

2. Verify the detail panel is still visible and positioned correctly

3. Scroll back to the left and verify panel position

Step 4: Test Multiple Panels

1. Open a second detail panel by clicking another invoice row

2. Scroll the table horizontally

3. Verify both panels maintain their sticky positioning
